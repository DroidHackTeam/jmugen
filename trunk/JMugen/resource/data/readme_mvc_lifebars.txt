---------------------------------------------------------
 Installation
---------------------------------------------------------

*Extract the files in a sub-folder in the data directory;

*open your screenpack's system.def and modify the line
which defines your default lifebars to this:

fight = [name of the sub-folder]/mvc_lifebars.def

*Run Mugen & enjoy.
